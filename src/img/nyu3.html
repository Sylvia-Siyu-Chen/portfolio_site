Inspired by the energy and creativity of the city it calls home, NYU Steinhardt wanted to expand its reach—touching lives of talented, altruistic people across the world. And in 2015, we teamed up to make it happen with four online degree programs.

Realizing the potential.

With NYU Steinhardt's mission of being a “top quality international center of scholarship, teaching, and research,” it made sense that the University would want to take some of their degree programs online. After all, NYU Steinhardt’s one-of-a-kind integration of education, communication, health, and the arts puts students at the heart of a movement—education for social change.

To help expand those efforts and reach more students across the world, we joined the effort. And together, we created four online degree programs, allowing them to transform communities and empower diverse students across the globe.

Faculty and fellow students have pushed me to be intellectually stimulated and genuinely curious to a degree I did not know I was capable of.
— Andy B., Student, Counseling@NYU
